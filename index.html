What is Qimen Dunjia?

Imagine having a cosmic GPS that tells you the best time and direction for important decisions. That's Qimen Dunjia.

Think of it like this:

Just as a GPS considers traffic, road conditions, and your destination to find the best route, Qimen Dunjia considers cosmic timing, directional energies, and your specific question to reveal optimal conditions for success.

Key Points:

  • Over 3,000 years old, used by Chinese emperors and generals
  • Combines astronomy, geography, and time cycles
  • Helps identify favorable timing and directions
  • Not fortune-telling — strategic planning with cosmic awareness

Wisdom, assistance, learning. Excellent for study and seeking help.

Tian Xin Star · 天心

Healing, spirituality, compassion. Good for health matters.

Loyalty, stability, reliability. Great for partnerships.

Tian Qin Star · 天禽

Balance, centeredness, neutrality. Stability and peace.

Tian Rui Star · 天芮

Illness, problems. Indicates areas needing caution.

荣格心理学视角

荣格曾为《太乙金华宗旨》作序，发现东方修炼与西方心理学的深刻联系

正念与觉察
荣格认为，静坐时的"观息"类似于心理学的"正念观察"，是培养觉察力的基础。东方修炼的本质是让意识与潜意识重新连接。
曼陀罗与光
眼前的"光"可被理解为"曼陀罗"的雏形——潜意识通过光的象征向意识传递信息。这是人类共通的深层心理现象。
元神与自性
"元神"类似于荣格所说的"自性"——人格的整体性与中心。当元神显现，意味着意识与潜意识达成和解，这就是"个性化"过程。
经典原文

"回光者，非回一身之精华，直回天地造化之阳神也。"

Returning the light is not merely gathering the body's essence—it is summoning the creative Yang Spirit of heaven and earth itself.

"心易走，即以气纯之；气易粗，即以心细之。"

When the mind wanders, use the breath to steady it. When the breath grows rough, use the mind to refine it.

"光是易散难聚的，心是易动难定。需行百日，其光乃真。"

Light easily scatters and is hard to gather. Practice for a hundred days, and the light becomes true.
